Eitan's Flashcard Studio

build, study, print & get quizzed on any word list

This app builds cards from a typed list. To get a list out of a photo or worksheet, use a free text-reading tool — then paste the result here.

1
Take a photo of your worksheet, or have the file ready on your device.
2
Ask an AI or OCR tool to read it. Open Claude, ChatGPT, Google Lens, or your phone's built-in text scanner, add the photo, and ask it to list the words. A prompt that works well:
Read this worksheet and list every word with its meaning, one pair per line, like: word — meaning
3
Copy the list it gives you and paste it in the box below. Give it a quick read to fix anything the reader got wrong — then send it to Build.
Have a PDF instead? If it's a normal PDF with selectable text (not a scan), drop it below and the words are pulled out automatically — no other tool needed.
Drop a PDF here, or tap to choose
Works for PDFs with real text · scanned PDFs need step 2 above

One card per line. Separate word and meaning with a dash, colon, comma, or tab — e.g. ephemeral — lasting a very short time. A word on its own works too.

Attach a photo to any card (optional)

Tap a card to flip its definition. Head to Print Cards to make a cut-out sheet.

Pick your options, then print. In your browser's print dialog: Scale: 100%, leave margins at their default ("Default" or "Normal" — not "None"). To save a PDF, choose "Save as PDF" as the destination.
